Introduction

When finding out what games you like, it's important to consider your interests and playstyle.
While most people have different preferences, there are some genres and consoles that are more popular for many.

Step 1: Choose Your Console

There are several consoles available for gaming. Each console can be very expensive, ranging from a few hundred to over a thousand dollars.

The Nintendo Switch 2 is more versatile in the sense that it is a handheld console that can also be used any time, anywhere. There is a dock, which connect a switch to any tv.
Playing on the go is convenient and allows you to game anywhere without needing a dedicated gaming setup.

On the other hand, the PlayStation 5 and Xbox Series X are more powerful consoles that offer better graphics and performance. They are designed for a more immersive gaming experience and are typically used in a dedicated gaming setup.
In contrast to that, the Nintendo Switch 2 offers a unique hybrid experience.

In a bigger picture, choosing the right console depends on your preferences, budget, and gaming style.

Step 2: Select Your Games

While choosing games, consider the genres you enjoy and the consoles on which they are available. There are hundreds of genres with different playing styles in them.

Some popular genres include action, adventure, role-playing, strategy, and sports. If you're new to gaming, you might want to start with more accessible games that have a lower learning curve.

Some popular games for beginners include "Minecraft," "Animal Crossing: New Horizons," and "Stardew Valley." These games are easy to pick up and offer a lot of fun without requiring a lot of gaming experience.

As you become more comfortable with gaming, you can explore more complex games and other genres. When finding new games to play, consider looking towards forums or communities dedicated to gaming, such as Neogaf or Reddit, where you can find recommendations and discussions about various games.

Genres for video games are categories based on the type of gameplay and themes they offer rather than a single defining characteristic. A game might be advertised as one genre but incorporate elements from others.

For example, "The Legend of Zelda: Breath of the Wild" is primarily an action-adventure game, but it also incorporates elements of role-playing and open-world exploration.

Step 3: Set Up Your Gaming Space

For a comfortable gaming experience, ensure you have a dedicated space with proper lighting, seating, and ventilation.

When playing using a PC computer, make sure to have a good monitor, keyboard, and mouse. For console gaming, a comfortable couch or chair and a large TV can enhance your experience.

Organize your gaming area to minimize distractions and create an immersive environment. Consider adding gaming accessories such as a headset for better audio and communication with other players.

Step 4: Join Gaming Communities

Among the gaming universe or multiverse, there are many communities where you can connect with other gamers, share experiences, and discuss gaming-related topics.
One of the popular platforms is Reddit, where you can find subreddits dedicated to specific games, genres, or anything that interests you. In most games, you are able to join servers, which allow you to play with other people online together like Minecraft. Another way to communicate with gamers alike is by using the platform Discord.
Discord is a popular communication platform that allows gamers to create servers, channels, and communities to connect with others who share similar interests.

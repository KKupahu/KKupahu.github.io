About Us| Island Brews Coffee

Island Brews was founded in 2019 with one goal: bringing the taste of hawaii in every cup
We are partnering with small farms on the Big Island to source 100 percent Kona coffee.

Brews Values

  • Community: We support local farmers
  • Sustainability: We use eco friendly packages and practice eco friendly methods
  • Aloha Spirit: We welcome everyone with aloha
  • Quality: We ensure every bean is roasted in small batches for freshness

Our Process

  1. Beans are hand picked on Hawaii Farms
  2. They are sun dried for 7-10 days
  3. All beans are roasted weekly in small batches at the local roastery
  4. Every cup is brewed fresh for all customers

Fun facts

  • Island brews was voted "Best Coffee Shop for three years in a row"
  • Our signature drink is "Volcanic Matcha" with Hawaii chili and dark chocolate
  • We go through 200 pounds of beans a week
  • Every wall has rotating artwork by local artists

Island Brews started as a small cart from a farmer's market,which has now grown to a neighborhood gathering spot.

Everyone should visit Island Brews because we are dedicated to bring the feeling of aloha to every single customer!

About Me

My name is Kaupo Kupahu and I'm a student at WCC. This website is my project for ICS 107.

I'm interested in web development and learning HTML. This site will grow throughout the semester.

I wanted to create a website that helps people understand what it means to be a gamer. This way, everyone is able to learn about gaming culture and its impact on society.

Gamer's Unite! Goals
Goal Description
Informative Content Provide accurate and engaging information about gaming culture, history, and trends.
Community Building Create a space for gamers to connect, share experiences, and discuss gaming-related topics.
